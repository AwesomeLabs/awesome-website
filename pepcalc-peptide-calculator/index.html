PepCalc

Simple peptide reconstitution
and dose calculator.

PepCalc is an easy to use peptide calculator that determines how many IUs or tick marks to measure in an insulin syringe of any size after reconstitution of research peptides. A great tool for measuring peptide doses.

  • App Store
  • Play Store

Features

  • Fully configurable - enter syringe volume, quantity of peptides, volume of sterile or bacteriostatic water, and desired dosage
  • Includes conversion tools to calculate between milligrams (MG), micrograms (MCG or ug), milliliters (mL), and microliters (uL)
  • No signup required and no ads!
