How it Works

We originally built Awesome Meal for ourselves to easily log our meals, monitor our intermittent fasting windows, and hold ourselves accountable. Now we're sharing it with you - and it works! Just open up the app, take a photo (or several photos) of your meal and you are done.

  • Snap photos of your meals (or select meal photos from your camera roll)
  • Optionally add a short description and/or tags
  • Forgot to take a photo? No problem! Just add a note-based meal log entry
  • Save, then view your meal journal on an interactive timeline
  • Close the app and get back to your life!

We came to the realization that the information already contained in meal photos provides a ton of useful information, such as:

  • Meal times (automatically extracted from photos)
  • Portion sizes
  • Variety of nutrients (food colors, vegetables, fruits, etc.)
  • Patterns and habits (always eating dinner late during the work week? Always having an afternoon coffee?)

Other ways you can use Awesome Meal:

  • Intermittent fasting? No problem! See where your meals fall in your eating window
  • Mindfully track your caffeine, water, or alcohol consumption
  • Track your supplements in relation to meals and time of day

We hope Awesome Meal helps you reach your meal tracking and intermittent fasting goals. We look forward to being a part of your journey!
