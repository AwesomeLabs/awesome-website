How it Works

We originally built Awesome for ourselves to easily track meals and hold ourselves accountable. Now we're sharing it with you. And it works! Pull up the app, take a photo (or several photos) of your meal and you are done.

  • Snap photos of your meals (or select meal photos from your camera roll)
  • Optionally add a short description and/or tags
  • Forgot to take a photo? No problem! Just add a note-based meal log entry
  • Save, then view your meal journal on an interactive timeline
  • Close the app and get back to your life!

We hope Awesome helps you reach your meal tracking goals, and look forward to being a part of your journey!
